The Cambrian Explosion was a period of time about 530 Million years ago when the rate of evolution seemed to accelerate and create a large diversity of new species in a relatively short amount of time.

The primary focus is to evolve locomotion in a variety of creatures using a genetic algorithm in a fun easily accessible simulation allowing to understand the fundamentals of evolution. Stay tuned. More to come :)
